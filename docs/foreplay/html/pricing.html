Questions? We have answers.

Still curious? Here are some of our frequently asked questions. If you can’t find your questions here, please contact our support.

Is Foreplay support available during my trial?

Definitely! Our popular live chat support is available for our customers and also for interested parties Monday to Friday from 9:00am - 5:00pm EST. Of course you can also reach our team by email. Contact us for any kind of questions and just write to hello@foreplay.co

Do I get support & help as a customer?

Yes, and very much so. In our software, you'll find the help articles that help you step by step.  They will always help you if you get stuck. Also, our team can assist you within minutes via chat.

Can I cancel at anytime?

Yes, if you signup for a Foreplay account and decide the platform isn't for you, you can cancel your monthly subscription. However, if you commit to a yearly plan you will not receive a refund for your cancelation.

How does the trial work? Will I be charged?

When you sign up for a trial of Foreplay you will select a plan to trial for 7 days and enter a credit card. After your 7-day free trial you will be automatically subscribed to the plan you are trialing and your credit card will be charged. During your 7-day trial you can cancel your trial to opt-out of auto-billing. We understand some people might forget to do this is Foreplay isn't for them, we offer a 14-day money back guarentee after your first charge.

Are there any usage limitations?

There are no usage limitations on any of our plans. You can save unlimited ads from Facebook Ad Library, TikTok Creative Center and more.

Do you offer annual discounts?

Yes! All paid plans are available for ~15% off the monthly price if you pay annually. There is no pro-rated refunds for annual commitments.
